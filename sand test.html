The Kimball Story

Family-Owned,
Community-Rooted

"

Kimball Sand Company, Inc., founded in 1979, (serving Central/Eastern Massachusetts and Northern Rhode Island) is a family-owned-and-operated stone and gravel company. Kimball Sand offers a variety of top-quality aggregate products for construction, building, and development projects, as well as weekend-warrior undertakings. We offer delivery and a variety of payment options. At Kimball Sand, you are sure to find a knowledgeable and friendly staff to assist you in the selection and delivery of the top-quality product that best fits your project needs.

Through economic cycles, changing industry standards, and decades of growth, Kimball Sand has remained committed to what matters most: sourcing the best material from our own pits, delivering it reliably, and standing behind every load we sell.

Milestones
1979
The Kimball family founds Kimball Sand Company with a single truck and opens the first pit operation in Blackstone, Massachusetts.
1985
Fleet expansion doubles delivery capacity. Kimball Sand begins serving Rhode Island contractors and municipal accounts.
1994
Second facility opens in Northborough, MA, dramatically improving access for clients across Worcester County and the MetroWest region.
2005
Kimball Sand invests in modern screening and crushing equipment, enabling production of a broader range of specialty aggregate products.
2015
Service area expands to select regions of New Hampshire and Vermont, meeting regional demand from longtime commercial clients.
Today
Over 45 years later, Kimball Sand remains family-owned, with a fleet of 15+ trucks, three locations, and a reputation built on delivering what we promise.
